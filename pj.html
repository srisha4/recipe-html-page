Delicious Creamy Pasta Recipe

About the Dish

This creamy pasta is a quick and easy dish perfect for lunch or dinner. It is rich, flavourful, and can be prepared in less than 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water for 8–10 minutes.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and sauté garlic until slightly golden.
  3. Add fresh cream and stir well.
  4. Mix in black pepper and salt.
  5. Add boiled pasta and mix until coated evenly.
  6. Cook for 2 more minutes on low flame.
  7. Top with grated cheese and serve hot.

Final Look

Tips & Variations

  • Add vegetables like corn, spinach, broccoli, or mushrooms.
  • Use any pasta you prefer – penne, spaghetti, fusilli.
  • Add chili flakes if you want a spicy version.
